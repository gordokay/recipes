Scrambled Eggs

Description

They are good

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked paprika
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Set stove top to medium-low heat
  2. Line pan with butter
  3. Mix eggs in bowl with salt, pepper, and smoked paprika
  4. Cook to desired state, pushing eggs around the pan randomly
